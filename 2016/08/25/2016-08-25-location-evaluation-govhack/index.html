Location Evaluation (GovHack 2016)

Due to the Melbourne public security is becoming much worse, there are lots of people like me worrying about the place where we live in. Whether it is safe enough or not. Also for those people who is seeking for the new house, the security of that is a critical part before they make the final decision.

So what came in my mind is that why not we just create an app to evaluate the property address! And it could be a reference for people to consider.

Currently, there are two elements involved to generate the final report score, which includes transportation and security.

The security score is mainly calculated from the government criminal record.

The convenient score is based on other three interest place addresses. By calculating the travel time between the three paths, the average of that is the convenient score.

The structure of app is a pure javascript program, and there is no back end server to support. It can run in any client browser directly.
